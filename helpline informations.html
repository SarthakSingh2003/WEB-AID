Emergency Services in India

Important Emergency Numbers

  • Police: 100
  • Fire: 101
  • Ambulance: 102 / 108
  • Women Helpline: 1091
  • Child Helpline: 1098
  • Senior Citizens Helpline: 1291
  • Natural Disaster Helpline: 1078
  • Railway Accident Emergency: 1072
  • Road Accident Emergency: 1073
  • Air Ambulance: 9540161344

SOS Queries

If you have urgent queries or need immediate help, reach out using the following contacts:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741

Helpful Organizations

Connect with these organizations for emergency services and support:

  • National Disaster Response Force (NDRF)
  • Indian Red Cross Society
  • St. John Ambulance India
  • Central Industrial Security Force (CISF)
  • Indian Coast Guard
  • National Health Mission (NHM)
  • Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR)
