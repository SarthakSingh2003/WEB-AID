Medication Management

Ensure proper management of medications to promote better sleep. Consult with your healthcare provider to understand any potential impacts on your sleep.

  • Helpline: 1-800-MED-HELP

Emotional Wellbeing

Prioritize your emotional wellbeing. Practice relaxation techniques, mindfulness, or engage in activities that promote mental health to create a conducive environment for quality sleep.

  • Helpline: 1-800-EMO-HELP
  • Website: EmotionalWellbeing.org
  • Therapist: Dr. Sarah Johnson
  • Video: Emotional Wellbeing Tips

Common Old Age Problems

Address common old age problems that may affect sleep, such as joint pain or discomfort. Consider adaptive solutions to enhance your sleep environment.

  • Arthritis Helpline: 1-888-555-ARTR
  • Fall Prevention: FallSafety.org
  • Geriatric Specialist: Dr. Jane Miller
  • Video: Understanding Old Age Problems

Regular Health Checkups

Schedule regular health checkups to monitor and address any underlying health issues that could impact your sleep. Early detection and management are crucial for overall well-being.

  • Annual Checkup: HealthCheckup.com
  • Preventive Care: PreventiveCare.org
